# 🎨 PromptFill - Simplify Your AI Art Prompts

## 🚀 What is PromptFill?
PromptFill is a structured prompt generation tool designed specifically for AI art applications like Nano Banana. It helps you quickly build, manage, and iterate complex prompts through a visual “fill-in-the-blank” interaction. Whether you're an artist or just exploring, PromptFill makes prompt creation straightforward and enjoyable.

## ⚙️ Features
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Navigate easily with a clean layout.
- **Visual Editing:** Interact with prompts in a fillable format.
- **Prompt Management:** Save and organize your prompts for later use.
- **Real-Time Feedback:** See how changes influence the generated output instantly.
- **Export Options:** Download your finalized prompts to use in your favorite AI art tools.

## 📦 System Requirements
- **Operating System:** Windows 10 or later, macOS Mojave or later.
- **RAM:** Minimum 4GB recommended for optimal performance.
- **Storage:** At least 100MB of free disk space.
- **Internet Connection:** Required for updates and downloading additional features.

## 🗂️ How to Use PromptFill
1. **Launch the Application:** Open PromptFill from your applications folder.
2. **Start a New Prompt:** Click on “New Prompt” to begin.
3. **Fill in the Blanks:** Use the visual interface to input details for your prompt. 
4. **Preview Your Prompt:** Click “Preview” to see a live generation based on your input.
5. **Save Your Prompt:** Once satisfied, save your prompt by clicking “Save.”
6. **Export if Needed:** You can export your prompt to share with others or for use in AI art tools.
